About Our College

About Maria College of Engineering and Technology (MCET, Kanyakumari) Accordion Icon V3 Established in 2008, Maria College of Engineering and Technology (MCET, Kanyakumari) is a premier Institute located in Tamil Nadu. Institute offers Degree courses including 7 UG and 9 PG courses. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. Maria College of Engineering and Technology (MCET, Kanyakumari) is a premier institute for B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, M.E./M.Tech courses. Candidate can pursue in multiple range of courses like B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, M.E./M.Tech with the stream of Engineering, Business & Management Studies. Over the period, Maria College of Engineering and Technology (MCET, Kanyakumari) has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Computer Science Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Aeronautical Engineering, Automobile Engineering, Finance, Communications Engineering, Power Engineering, Structural Engineering. Courses offered by institute are recognized by approving bodies like AICTE. Maria College of Engineering and Technology (MCET, Kanyakumari) offered courses for 702 seats. Courses offered by the Institute are in the fee range of INR 35,000-200,000 for a total of 702 candidates. Maria College of Engineering and Technology (MCET, Kanyakumari) provide good facilities all around the campus like Gym, Hostel, Library, Shuttle Service, Sports Complex.

VISION

  • Admission to the undergraduate courses is based on the marks obtained in TNEA conducted by the Anna University.
  • Candidates have to apply through the offline mode
  • Candidate applying for the undergraduate level courses should have passed the 2nd PUC/Class XII with English, Physics, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects and obtained a minimum aggregate of 50% in Physics, Mathematics (compulsory subjects) with one of the optional subject
  • To apply for the postgraduate level courses, candidates must be a graduate and have a bachelors degree.
  • Admission to undergraduate level courses will be done by the Anna University on the basis of TNEA score.
  • To be eligible for M.E. courses, candidates must have a valid GATE/CET score.
  • Admission to MBA courses will be based on PGECET/KMAT/NEET/CAT/MAT/XAT/TANCET Score.
